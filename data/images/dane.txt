The cryotherapy machine suddenly stops working due to a power outage caused by a severe thunderstorm that hit the city. The power surge caused a short circuit in the machine, which led to the temperature rising and the chamber thawing.

After Dane's awakening, he finds himself in a factory where he is disoriented and confused. As he tries to explore his surroundings, he accidentally gets his arm caught in a piece of machinery, which tears it off. The factory workers rush to his aid and call for emergency medical assistance, but unfortunately, Dane's arm cannot be reattached.
